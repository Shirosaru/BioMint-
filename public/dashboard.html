Why This Matters

🩸 Diabetes Detection
CGM streams (Dexcom, Libre) and routine HbA1c panels detect pre-diabetic patterns years before symptoms. Longitudinal glucose data is among the highest-value inputs for early metabolic risk models.
🧬 GLP-1 Pharmacogenomics
Tirzepatide and semaglutide work differently across genotypes. Pairing T2D GWAS loci (TCF7L2, KCNJ11, PPARG) with CGM response data powers the next generation of personalised GLP-1 prescribing.
💊 Antibody Therapeutics
Monoclonal antibody response varies hugely across patients. IgG titres, cytokine profiles (IL-6, TNF-α) and CD4 counts from immune panels let pharma companies — like AstraZeneca — predict mAb durability and stratify autoimmune flares.
🏥 Everyday Users Earn
You don't need a rare disease. A routine annual blood draw (HbA1c, CRP, lipids) can earn small but real SOL payments whenever those panels improve an inflammation or metabolic risk model. No wearable required.
📱 Lifestyle & Wearables
Apple Watch / Garmin / Nike paired with CGM reveals which lifestyle changes actually reduce post-meal spikes. Exercise, diet, sleep — all priced by their real model impact.
💡 The BioMint Guarantee
The hub owns nothing. Raw data stays on patient devices or encrypted vaults. Only Merkle hashes and pseudonymous IDs go on-chain. Payments are automatic, traceable, and fully signed on Solana. Bad data earns zero — the market is self-cleaning.
